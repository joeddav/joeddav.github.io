Hi, I'm Joe. I'm a PhD student at the University of Utah, advised by Vivek Srikumar. My research areas include machine learning, natural language processing, and artificial intelligence.

I used to be a member of the science team at Hugging Face, an open source startup that develops state-of-the-art NLP and machine learning tools. I also spent over a year as a Senior Machine Learning Scientist at Enveda Biosciences where I brought my Transformers expertise to problems like predicting full chemical structuress from mass spec.

I completed my master's in Data Science at Harvard in 2019 where I did research with Sasha Rush (commonsense and NLP) and with Finale Doshi-Velez (Bayesian Neural Networks). During my stint at Harvard, I interned with IBM Research where I developed a novel approach to disentanglement in VAEs.

You can find more details about my background in my CV.
